# Landform — Platform Guide

*Built for Cassidy Group by Phil Daniel. A plain-English map of every page, what it does, and the journey to follow for each type of deal.*

---

## 1. How the platform is organised

Landform is built around **journeys**. You tell the tool what kind of deal you're working on, and the sidebar automatically shows only the pages relevant to that deal — in the order you should work through them.

Every page belongs to one of **8 phases**:

| Phase | What it's for |
|-------|---------------|
| **0. Start** | Pick the deal type and map your route |
| **1. Find** | Source the site and screen it before you commit |
| **2. Value** | Work out what you can build and what it's worth |
| **3. Develop** | Planning strategy, the full appraisal, due diligence and risk |
| **4. Exit** | Decide who buys it and how you get your money out |
| **5. Report** | Produce the scorecard, teaser, memorandum and investor pack |
| **6. Records** | Dashboard, portfolio and meeting notes |
| **7. Audit** | Check every number is consistent; maintain cost benchmarks |

**The seven deal types (journeys):**

- **Land & Development** — strategic land: buy → planning → develop → exit
- **Single Family Housing (SFH)** — a plot-by-plot housing estate
- **Build to Rent (BTR)** — a multi-family rental block
- **PBSA** — purpose-built student accommodation
- **Property Evaluator** — an existing building you might keep, refurb or redevelop
- **Planning Recovery** — reviving a stalled or refused scheme
- **Asset Exit Optimiser** — finding the best way out of an asset you already own

You don't have to visit every page — the sidebar marks each one **Required**, **Recommended** or **Optional** for your chosen deal type, and some pages become *Required* automatically when they're relevant (e.g. **Grants** becomes required the moment you add affordable housing; **Capitalisation** becomes required if you retain homes for rent or sell in bulk to a pension fund).

## 2. The pages — what each one does

### Phase 0 — Start
- **🧭 Process Navigator** — A 3-step setup wizard. Pick your scheme type and likely exit routes, and it lays out a tailored, ordered checklist of every stage to complete, with progress tracking and decision checkpoints (e.g. *"Is the land value at least the asking price?"*) and jump-links into each page. **Start here.**
- **🏛 Asset Exit Optimiser** — A "best way out" calculator for an asset you already own or are about to complete. Enter the asset, its income, costs and debt, and your priority (cash, IRR, strategic, or low-risk), and it ranks every exit — sell, refinance, hold, change of use, split-sale — with value, net proceeds, yield and a recommendation.

### Phase 1 — Find
- **🤖 Placona Agent** — AI land sourcing. Pick counties and a minimum home count and it searches planning portals and agent listings, scores the sites it finds, and lets you load any one straight into your appraisal with one click.
- **📡 Planning Monitor** — A planning-intelligence watchlist for a council area. Tracks recent approvals, emerging allocations, grey-belt and Class Q opportunities, 5-year land supply, and a pro-development score for the LPA.
- **⚠ Constraint Check** — Rapid site screening that replaces checking a dozen government websites. Enter a postcode and get a read on Green Belt, flood, conservation, ecology etc., a planning-probability score out of 100, and a **GO / CAUTION / AVOID** verdict.
- **🔍 Land Finder** — Turns a land advert into a pre-filled deal. Paste a listing URL or the agent's text and it extracts the address, acreage, price, planning status and agent, then pre-fills your appraisal and estimates anything missing.
- **⬟ Land Appraisal** — The core site-scoring page. Enter the site details to get a 0–100 location/risk score, a land-value benchmark band based on planning status, and a five-scenario valuation (full consent / outline / allocated / likely / hope value). **Choosing a scenario here pushes its assumptions across the rest of the tool.**
- **🏠 Property Evaluator** — A "keep, refurb or redevelop" appraisal for an existing building. Values it as-is, estimates redevelopment potential, and compares *sell as standing* vs *sell with planning* vs *demolish & develop*.

### Phase 2 — Value
- **◆ Land Valuation (RLV)** — The residual valuation that tells you the **most you can pay for the land**. Pulls live Land Registry sold prices to set a sale £/sqft, then deducts build, fees, finance, S106, infrastructure and profit from GDV. Includes sensitivity sliders, a "make it stack" solver and a RICS-style report.
- **🏡 SFH House Mix** — The estate-housing appraisal. Enter acres, density, sale and build £/sqft and your house-type mix (with tenure per row); it gives blended GDV, the full cost stack, residual land value per plot and per acre, margin and net profit.
- **🤝 Tenure Mix** — Plans the split across tenures (open market, affordable/social rent, shared ownership, BTR, First Homes). Shows blended GDV vs pure open-market GDV, the discount, rental income, and a buyer-by-buyer roadmap of who buys what.
- **🏢 BTR / PBSA Block** — The flatted-block appraisal. From the building's geometry and unit mix it derives unit count, GDV, the full high-rise cost stack (lifts, sprinklers, cladding/EWS1, Building Safety Act gateways), residual land value and margin — and values the block both as *flats sold individually* and *sold to an investor on the rent*.
- **£ Capitalisation** — The investment-value calculator for rented or bulk-sold schemes. Takes bedroom mix and rents, running-cost deductions and the **net initial yield** to produce net income (NOI) and capitalised value at every buyer type. Also aggregates the SFH mix by sale route (private / pension bulk / affordable / retained rent) and shows blended realisable GDV, costs and profit per route.
- **💷 Grant & Funding** — Scores eligibility and likely amounts across UK grant programmes (Affordable Homes Programme, Brownfield Land Release Fund, HIF, remediation relief), models the funding gap, and drafts a grant application pack and an RP introduction email.

### Phase 3 — Develop
- **▲ Planning & Viability** — Planning strategy and policy-compliance tracker. Record the LPA, status, units, storeys, affordable %, S106/CIL and biodiversity/fire position; it produces a compliance checklist (NPPF, Local Plan, BNG, Gateway, S106), flags mismatches with other pages, and routes refused schemes to Recovery.
- **◉ Financial Modelling** — The headline development appraisal: full cost-vs-GDV, profit, margin and return on cost, with bear/base/bull scenarios, a 12-line S106 calculator, a simplified IRR, phased cashflow with peak debt, Excel export, and a landowner-vs-Cassidy split of the uplift.
- **📐 Detailed Appraisal** — The institutional-grade residual appraisal: revenue by tenure, itemised infrastructure, CIL/S106, finance and target margins, with an honest-vs-headline margin, £/acre, a monthly cashflow giving true peak debt and the first cash-positive month.
- **◈ Due Diligence** — A structured tick-list across Legal, Technical, Planning and Commercial, with completion tracking and an AI gap analysis on what's outstanding before exchange.
- **⬡ Risk Register** — A live red/amber/green risk log. Add risks with mitigations; the page tallies them and gives a risk-adjusted proceed / don't-proceed recommendation.

### Phase 4 — Exit
- **◆ Exit Strategy** — *"What's it worth and how do I get out?"* Runs the scheme through five buyer-type valuation engines (housebuilder, BTR fund, PBSA fund, housing association, pension fund), shows what each would pay and their appetite, compares hold vs sell vs refinance, tracks actual RP offers, and AI-generates Heads of Terms and an investment memo.
- **⚖ Planning Recovery** — A workflow for buying refused land cheaply and getting it consented. Record the refusal reason, knockdown price and consented value; it presents recovery routes (appeal, resubmission, Local Plan promotion), case law, an action plan and an AI letter generator.

### Phase 5 — Report
- **🏆 Site Scorecard** — Rates the site 1–10 across seven dimensions (viability, planning, market, location, constraint risk, delivery, return) and rolls them into a **STRONG BUY / BUY / CONDITIONAL / PASS** verdict with investment-committee commentary.
- **📬 Teaser PDF** — A confidential 1–2 page branded teaser of the headline figures, for pitching a site to investors or RPs.
- **📑 Investor Memorandum** — A full institutional IM, AI-built across seven standard sections, exported as a branded PDF for post-NDA distribution.
- **📁 Data Room** — A virtual data room with a security-first audience toggle: an **Internal** view shows everything (walk-away prices, internal margins, red flags), while the **External** view strips internal-only content and rounds sensitive figures — producing a safe-to-share investor pack.
- **🎯 Investor Marketing Suite** — The outreach control centre. Wraps the Teaser, IM and Data Room into a marketing funnel, creates trackable per-investor share links (with passcode/expiry), and reports who's viewed what.
- **📄 Executive Summary** — A one-page roll-up of the whole deal pulled from every stage.

### Phase 6 — Records
- **📝 Meeting Transcripts** — Upload or paste transcripts/minutes/spreadsheets; AI extracts attendees, decisions, action items and deal impacts into a structured summary.
- **📊 Deal Portfolio** — Your cloud library of saved deals (personal or team), with open/delete/share to named teammates.
- **◈ Deal Dashboard** — The deal home screen: live headline metrics (GDV, dev cost, profit/margin after land, NOI, yield, units), a reconciliation of target vs actual margin, the Go/No-Go verdict, and a checklist of which stages still need filling.

### Phase 7 — Audit
- **🔬 Propagation Audit** — A consistency checker that maps every key assumption (units, affordable %, S106, finance rate, build/sale, yield, GDV) across all pages and flags anything out of sync — with one-click "Sync" / "Auto-fix all" to push the master value everywhere.
- **🧱 Build Cost Library** — Where the QS maintains the BCIS-style £/sqft build rates by scheme and house type, plus a Tier-1 contractor uplift. These feed the "Auto-cost" buttons across every appraisal.

*Behind the scenes there are also three helper workflows — a **Land Workflow** (greenfield negotiation guide + scheme comparison), a **Property/EPE Workflow** (demolish-and-rebuild scheme comparison), and **Flowcharts** (clickable process maps) — plus a **Media Library** for site photos and videos attached to a deal.*

## 3. The journeys — what follows what

Below is the recommended order for each deal type. Decision points are marked **◆**.

### 🏡 Single Family Housing (a housing estate)
> *Find land → value the estate → develop → exit → market it.*

1. **Process Navigator** — pick "SFH" and your likely exit routes
2. **Land Finder / Placona / Constraint Check / Planning Monitor** — source and screen the site
3. **Land Appraisal** — score the site, pick the planning scenario ◆ *Is the land value ≥ asking price?*
4. **Land Valuation (RLV)** — confirm the most you can pay for the land
5. **SFH House Mix** — set your plots, house types, sizes, prices and tenure → gives GDV, RLV per plot, margin
6. **Tenure Mix** — split open-market vs affordable/rent (auto-feeds the mix)
7. **Capitalisation** — *only if* you retain homes for rent or bulk-sell to a pension fund — gives the income value
8. **Grant & Funding** — *required once you have affordable housing* — find grant to close the gap
9. **Planning & Viability** — planning strategy and policy compliance
10. **Financial Modelling → Detailed Appraisal** — the headline appraisal, then the institutional version
11. **Due Diligence → Risk Register** — checklist and risk log before exchange
12. **Exit Strategy** — who buys it (open-market plots, RP for affordable, pension for retained) ◆ *Which exit maximises value?*
13. **Site Scorecard** — the buy/pass verdict
14. **Teaser → Investor Memorandum → Data Room → Investor Marketing Suite** — produce and distribute the investor pack
15. **Executive Summary / Deal Dashboard** — final roll-up

### 🏢 Build to Rent (a rental block)
1. **Process Navigator** (pick BTR) → 2. **Find** pages → 3. **Land Appraisal** → 4. **RLV**
5. **Tenure Mix** → 6. **BTR / PBSA Block** (the block appraisal) → 7. **Capitalisation** (income value — the primary GDV for BTR) → 8. **Grant & Funding** (if affordable)
9. **Planning & Viability** → 10. **Financial Modelling → Detailed Appraisal** → 11. **Due Diligence → Risk Register**
12. **Exit Strategy** (forward-fund / forward-sale / stabilise & sell) → 13. **Site Scorecard**
14. **Teaser → IM → Data Room → Investor Marketing Suite** → 15. **Executive Summary / Dashboard**

### 🎓 PBSA (student accommodation)
Same as BTR, but step 5 is skipped (no tenure split) — go **RLV → BTR/PBSA Block → Capitalisation → Grants → Planning → Financial Modelling → Detailed Appraisal → DD → Risks → Exit → reports.** The block page applies student-specific costs and per-bed values.

### ⬟ Land & Development (strategic land, no build)
1. **Process Navigator** (pick Land) → 2. **Find** pages → 3. **Land Appraisal** → 4. **RLV** (the key output — what to pay)
5. **Capitalisation** (optional, if testing an income exit) → 6. **Grant & Funding** (optional)
7. **Planning & Viability** → 8. **Due Diligence → Risk Register**
9. **Exit Strategy** (sell the consented land to a housebuilder/investor) → 10. **Site Scorecard**
11. **Teaser → IM → Data Room → Investor Marketing Suite** → 12. **Executive Summary / Dashboard**

### 🏠 Property Evaluator (an existing building)
1. **Process Navigator** (pick Property) → 2. **Property Evaluator** (keep / refurb / redevelop) → 3. **RLV**
4. **SFH House Mix / Tenure Mix** — *if* you're redeveloping into housing
5. **Planning & Viability → Financial Modelling** → 6. **Due Diligence**
7. **Exit Strategy** → 8. **Data Room → Investor Marketing Suite** → 9. **Executive Summary / Dashboard**

### ⚖ Planning Recovery (a refused/stalled scheme)
1. **Process Navigator** (pick Recovery) → 2. **Planning & Viability** (record the refusal) → 3. **Financial Modelling** (the discounted-buy maths)
4. **Due Diligence → Risk Register** → 5. **Exit Strategy**
6. **Planning Recovery** (the recovery route, case law, appeal/letters) → 7. **Executive Summary / Dashboard**

### 🏛 Asset Exit Optimiser (an asset you already own)
1. **Process Navigator** → 2. **Asset Exit Optimiser** (rank every exit route) → 3. **Investor Marketing Suite** (if selling)

## 4. Two things that run across every journey

- **Enter once, flows everywhere.** Key numbers (city, units, affordable %, S106, build/sale £/sqft, finance rate, **net initial yield**) are shared — type them on one page and they populate the others. The **Propagation Audit** is your safety net: it flags anything that's drifted out of sync and fixes it in one click.
- **One source of truth per number.** The appraisal uses a single canonical engine, so the GDV and residual land value you see on the Dashboard are the same ones in the SFH/Block appraisal, the Exit page and the reports — they always reconcile.
